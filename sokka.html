Sokka

He is the brother of Katara
Sokka is a non-bender, but as the show progresses he masters swordplay and proves himself to be a worthy and intelligent strategist
Sokka uses different weapons such as his boomerang and machete; and eventually a jian sword to combat enemies.

Bio

fifteen-year-old Sokka is a warrior of the Southern Water Tribe, a nation where some people are able to telekinetically manipulate, or "bend", water.[2] He, along with his younger sister Katara, discovers an Airbender named Aang, the long-lost Avatar, and accompanies him to defeat the imperialistic Fire Nation and bring peace to the war-torn nations.
